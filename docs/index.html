About The Project

Immerse yourself in a groundbreaking VR experience that sheds light on the alarming issue of plastic pollution in rivers. Narrated by a seasoned fisherman, our virtual journey takes you through three distinct scenes of a river:

  1. The Clean Source
  2. Trash Accumulation in the Middle
  3. High Plastic Levels at the End

Experience stunning visuals and captivating audio as you witness the transformation of a pristine river into a polluted waterway. This project aims to raise awareness about the significant contribution of rivers to the plastic crisis in our seas.
